Mararet Hamilton

Margaret Hamilton was a woman. Born in 1936, she lived through World War II and the Cold War, up until the present. She has seen the floppy disk come and go along with many other things. She could tell me so many interesting stories, which is what made me interested in writing about her. While looking her up, I found out that she actually studied math, which I rarely find amusing or interesting. So my faith in becoming like her plummeted.
But I continued reading.
